The profile of Insuk (Chris) Cho

Last updated: June 5, 2018

Welcome, I am Chris Cho.

Chris is more than 15 years experienced Software Engineer w/ bachelor of Computer Engineering in South Korea. Most of the experience is based on B2B enterprise project in finance, manufacturing and power plants using Java & Spring framework. Currently, he has the interest in machine learning algorithms for prediction future abnormal behavior at the power plant. And also working as a full-stack developer using lots of JavaScript frameworks like Backbone, D3, RxJS and so on. He loves Agile software development environment like Scrum, TDD, pair programming, CI/CD, lean product development and working with the cross-functional team as an Agile/Lean evangelist. Now, he is working for Elastic as a technical Sr. Support Engineer.

He loves to share what he has learned, so he wrote 2 books talking about python programming for novice programmer and Node.js programming for the actual business field. And also trying to write helpful articles for making better software development environment in South Korea. If you have interest, check this blog.

He has great experience to work with global top engineers @ Pivotal Labs San Francisco office as a product manager and a developer and also with great engineers from Germany, U.K, Belarus, India and so on. Currently, he keeps looking for great opportunity to work with the advanced global top engineers to help and learn their business as a senior software engineer with agile experience.
