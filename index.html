Chris is more than 12 years experienced Software Engineer w/ bachelor of Computer Engineering in South Korea. Currently, he has the interest in machine learning algorithms for prediction future abnormal behavior at the power plant. And also working as a full-stack developer using lots of JavaScript frameworks like Backbone, D3, RxJS and so on. He loves Agile software development environment like Scrum, TDD, pair programming, CI/CD, lean product development and working with the cross-functional team as an Agile/Lean evangelist.

He loves to share what he has learned, so he wrote 2 books talking about python programming for novice programmer and Node.js programming for the actual business field. And also trying to write helpful articles for making better software development environment in South Korea. If you have interest, check this blog.

He has great experience to work with global top engineers @ Pivotal Labs San Francisco office as a product manager and a developer and also with great engineers from Germany, U.K, Belarus, India and so on. Currently, he keeps looking for great opportunity to work with the advanced global top engineers to help and learn their business as a senior software engineer with agile experience.
